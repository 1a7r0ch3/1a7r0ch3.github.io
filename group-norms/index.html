Methods for Group Norms Manipulations

Methods in C or C++ or MATLAB/Octave for manipulating group norms, popular in inverse problems for signal processing or in machine learning.
Methods in C or C++ are provided with the MEX API for use within MATLAB/Octave. Implementations make efficient use of the OpenMP API for parallel computation.

ℓ1,p-norms refer to norms defined as weighted sums of ℓp-norms over groups of coordinates, inducing certain structure on the vector of interest.
δ1,p-semi-norms refer to semi-norms defined as weighted sums of δp-semi-norms over groups of coordinates, which somewhat evaluate the "spreading" of the values within a group.
Note that a special case of δ1,2-semi-norm constitutes a discretization of the total variation semi-norm, with better computational and isotropic properties than usual discretizations.
They are all described extensively in chapters IV and V of my Ph.D. thesis.

For now, the codes are provided without further documentation. For more information, feel free to contact me*.
